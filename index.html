Welcome to The Ocean.

The aim of this game is to test your memory.
Click on 2 cards.
If they match they will dissapear.
If not they will turn back over.
Match all the cards until there are none left.
You can choose your level of difficulty by clicking either Easy, Medium or Hard buttons.

Use landscape mode if playing on your phone.

Have Fun!
